PASHU AADHAAR AI — 3-MINUTE PRESENTATION SCRIPT
================================================

[OPENING — 20 seconds]

India has 535 million livestock animals.
Less than 12% are formally identified.
Every year, the government loses over ₹15,000 crore to ghost animals in subsidy schemes,
duplicate insurance claims, and unverified loans against cattle.

We asked one question — if every Indian citizen has an Aadhaar,
why doesn't every Indian animal?

This is Pashu Aadhaar AI.

---

[THE PROBLEM — 20 seconds]

Today, a farmer in rural Andhra Pradesh has no way to prove ownership of his cow.
An insurer has no way to verify the animal actually exists before paying a claim.
A government officer has no real-time data on herd health, fraud, or enrollment trends.
And a veterinarian keeps paper records that disappear in a flood.

The system is broken — not because people don't care,
but because there was never a scalable, technology-driven solution.

Until now.

---

[THE SOLUTION — 30 seconds]

Pashu Aadhaar AI is a full-stack livestock identity platform that gives every animal
a unique biometric digital identity — just like Aadhaar for humans.

A field agent opens the app on their Android device or web browser.
They point the camera at the animal.
Our on-device AI — powered by YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art object detection model —
identifies the cow in real time, captures both the face and the unique muzzle print,
which is as unique to a cow as a fingerprint is to a human.

The images are processed through a Siamese neural network trained with metric learning.
This generates a biometric embedding — a mathematical signature of that animal —
stored in Amazon OpenSearch for instant similarity search.

In under 10 seconds, the animal has a permanent, tamper-proof digital identity.

---

[AI VALUE — 25 seconds]

AI is not a buzzword here — it is the core of the product.

Without AI, this is just a database.
With AI, it becomes a system that can:
— Detect duplicate animals across states in milliseconds using cosine similarity scoring
— Flag fraudulent enrollments automatically using multi-factor fraud scoring
  (biometric consistency, location, enrollment speed, agent behavior patterns)
— Run entirely on a mobile device without internet — model inference happens on-device
  using ONNX Runtime Web, so it works even in rural areas with poor connectivity

This is where AI directly adds value to every user:
  • For the farmer — proof of ownership, faster loans, better insurance premiums
  • For the government — real-time fraud detection before money leaves the treasury
  • For the insurer — verified animal identity before any policy is issued
  • For the veterinarian — complete health history at a QR scan

---

[KEY FEATURES — 30 seconds]

The platform serves 6 roles — farmer, enrollment agent, veterinarian, insurer,
government officer, and admin — each with a tailored dashboard.

Key capabilities:
— Biometric enrollment with dual capture: face + muzzle
— QR code identity cards — scan in the field with no login required
— Full health record tracking: vaccinations, diagnoses, treatments
— Daily milk yield monitoring with fat and SNF percentages
— Insurance policy management linked directly to the animal's biometric identity
— Government analytics dashboard with an interactive India map,
  state-level drill-down, enrollment trends, breed distribution, and agent leaderboards
— 7 Indian languages supported — because rural India does not speak English

---

[AWS ARCHITECTURE — 20 seconds]

Everything runs serverless on AWS:
— Amazon Cognito for secure, OTP-verified authentication via SNS
— API Gateway + AWS Lambda — 8 functions, zero servers to manage
— Amazon S3 for image storage, CloudFront for global CDN delivery
— DynamoDB for sub-millisecond data access at any scale
— Amazon SageMaker for hosting and serving AI models
— OpenSearch for vector similarity search
— CloudWatch for real-time monitoring and logs

This architecture scales from 1 enrollment to 10 crore enrollments
with zero changes to infrastructure and near-zero cost when idle.

---

[SCALABILITY + REAL WORLD APPLICATIONS — 20 seconds]

This is not just for cattle.

The exact same architecture applies to:
— Poultry identification for export certification
— Wildlife conservation — tracking tigers, elephants, rhinos by biometrics
— Pet identity registries for urban India
— Horse racing — verifying animal identity before races
— Research — tracking animal populations across sanctuaries

And at the national scale — if state governments adopt this,
Pashu Aadhaar becomes the backbone of India's digital livestock economy,
connecting farmers directly to Kisan Credit Card schemes,
PMFBY crop insurance, and NABARD livestock loans — all verified, all fraud-proof.

---

[CLOSING — 15 seconds]

We did not build an app.
We built infrastructure for India's livestock economy —
combining on-device AI, cloud-scale biometrics, and role-aware governance
into a system that works for a farmer in a remote village
and a government officer in Delhi simultaneously.

Pashu Aadhaar AI — because every animal deserves an identity.

Thank you.
